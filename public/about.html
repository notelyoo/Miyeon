💙 KarmineCorp

I've proudly been an ultra of the prestigious KarmineCorp since 2020, back when it was still known as KametoCorp. Initially, I followed their journey from a distance, but my passion really took flight in 2021 when their dedication and achievements inspired me deeply.

Among the talented players who've captured my admiration, Canna and Vladi stand out particularly for their incredible skills and remarkable consistency.

I've had the thrilling experience of attending several viewing parties, cheering alongside fellow fans during the memorable RedBull League of Legends event in Paris in 2024, and even traveling to Berlin to support the team firsthand during the exhilarating 2025 LEC Winter Split.

🎤 K‑pop

My journey into the vibrant world of K‑pop began completely by chance when I stumbled upon Blackpink's explosive hit, How You Like That. Captivated immediately, I dove headfirst into this dynamic musical universe and soon found myself exploring countless other sensational girl groups such as Twice, Red Velvet, Aespa, NewJeans, and (G)-IDLE.

Over time, this initial curiosity transformed into a deep passion, leading me to attend numerous concerts. Experiencing my favorite groups perform live has been nothing short of magical, reinforcing my love for K-pop with each event.

🏎️ Formula 1

I've been an avid follower of Formula 1 since 2018, drawn immediately by the sheer speed, intense strategy, and unparalleled excitement of each race.

My unwavering support goes primarily to RedBull Racing, and I'm especially a big admirer of Max Verstappen, whose relentless determination and driving prowess continually amaze me.

Alongside RedBull, I also have a soft spot for Ferrari, particularly cheering for Charles Leclerc, and I hold deep respect and admiration for the legendary Lewis Hamilton.

Each Grand Prix weekend is an event I eagerly anticipate.
